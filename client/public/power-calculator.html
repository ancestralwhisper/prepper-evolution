Power Planning Tips
Add 20% buffer — Real-world power draw is often higher than rated wattage. Inverter efficiency, temperature, and aging batteries all reduce usable capacity.
Solar extends everything — A 200W solar panel can replenish 600-1000Wh per day in good sun, dramatically extending your runtime.
LiFePO4 is king — LFP batteries last 3,000+ cycles vs 500 for standard lithium-ion. They're safer, more stable in heat, and the clear choice for preparedness.
Night drain matters — CPAP machines, mini fridges, and security cameras run 8-12 hours overnight. Factor these into your daily calculation.
